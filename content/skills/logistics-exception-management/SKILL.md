# Logistics Exception Management

## 輸入

- shipment id
- exception type
- customer SOP
- latest status
- promised ETA
- available evidence

## 輸出

- severity
- likely cause
- next action
- owner
- customer draft
- follow-up deadline

## 安全限制

- 不承諾賠償。
- 不保證到貨時間。
- 客戶可見訊息需要人工確認。

## English Summary

Classify logistics exceptions and prepare reviewable action plans and customer drafts.
